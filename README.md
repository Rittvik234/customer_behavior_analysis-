# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

> Turning raw retail data into actionable business insights using Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI.

## 📖 Project Overview

Businesses collect massive amounts of customer purchase data, but extracting meaningful insights from it can be challenging. This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to uncover purchasing patterns, customer preferences, subscription trends, and revenue-driving factors.

Using a dataset of **3,900 customer transactions**, I built an end-to-end analytics pipeline that transforms raw data into interactive business intelligence dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to answer questions such as:

* Which customers generate the most revenue?
* Do subscriptions increase customer spending?
* Which products perform best?
* How effective are discounts?
* Which customer segments should be targeted for marketing campaigns?

This project addresses these questions through data analysis and visualization.

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation (Python)

* Imported and explored customer transaction data.
* Handled missing values.
* Standardized column names.
* Created derived features such as:

  * Age Groups
  * Purchase Frequency Metrics
* Prepared data for database storage.

### 2️⃣ Database Analysis (PostgreSQL)

Loaded the cleaned dataset into PostgreSQL and performed SQL-based business analysis including:

* Revenue analysis by gender
* Subscription vs non-subscription spending
* Top-rated products
* Customer segmentation
* Discount impact analysis
* Age-group revenue contribution
* Repeat customer behavior

### 3️⃣ Dashboard Development (Power BI)

Built an interactive dashboard to help stakeholders explore:

📈 Revenue Trends

👥 Customer Segments

💳 Subscription Insights

🛒 Product Performance

🎯 Discount Effectiveness

🚚 Shipping Preferences

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### Customer Loyalty Matters

Returning and loyal customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue.

### Subscription Drives Revenue

Subscribed customers show higher average spending compared to non-subscribers.

### Product Ratings Influence Sales

Highly rated products consistently achieve stronger sales performance.

### Discount Dependency

Certain products rely heavily on discounts, highlighting opportunities for pricing optimization.

### Age-Based Spending Patterns

Specific age groups contribute disproportionately to overall revenue, enabling targeted marketing strategies.
